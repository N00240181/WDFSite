EventUs is the quintessential app for those looking to get more in touch with their college communities.

The app allows students to find out about any events that are going on in the college, where they can then RSVP and attend the event.

It also allows them to learn about clubs and societies that are in the college, so students can try new things and meet people.

You can also message your friends and create groups for your events and societies!

The calendar displays all the events for a given day, this allows students to plan ahead.

FAQ:

How much does it cost?

The application is free! The cost is subsidized by the participating colleges. There will never be any advertisements or any premium features, we believe an app like this should be available to everyone regardless of how much they spend.

What data is collected?

The only data we collect is the data you give us. This includes your email, name, and college. This data will never be shared with third parties, and is only kept to maintain the app’s functionality. All of the data related to you is deleted when you delete your account. If you would like a copy of your data please send us an email.

What colleges are supported?

Currently we only support colleges located in the Republic of Ireland, but we are looking to partner with other colleges within the European Union. If you are the head of the college and would like to be included please send an email to us.

What is your privacy policy?

We understand that users may share important information that they wish to keep private, this is why we keep all of your messages and personal data encrypted. Even our administrators can not see your data without your password, which is also encrypted.

Why did you create this app?

We created this app in the hopes of getting students to become more aware of what’s going on in their college communities. Many of us don’t know when or where events are taking place, and our own college’s site displaying the list of clubs and societies is archaic so we wanted to modernize the experience.

How accessible is the app?

We have tried to follow the WCAG guidelines closely while creating the application. Our app has many features like bold text, text to speech, dark mode, high contrast mode and much more. It’s vital to us that we allow all students to be included.
